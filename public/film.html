The Best Movies According to Mai

My top 3 movies of 2024.

1. Dune Part 2

Masterclass acting, mind bending cinematography, brilliant writing, and overall astounding storytelling can be found in Denis Villeneuve's “Dune: Part Two.”

2. Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga

This is an action film. I love the strong women in this movie. It's motivated. And it's glorious to behold thanks to the use of practical effects, incredible stunt work and stunning cinematography.

3. One Life

As it stands, One Life is a fairly unremarkable film about a truly remarkable man. Fresh score. The tear-jerker film that is One Life is an excellent ode to a model of non-violent heroism.
